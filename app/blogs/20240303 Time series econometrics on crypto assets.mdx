# Time series econometrics on crypto assets

I test for auto-correlation in the price of Bitcoin and Etherum, then model the price using ARMA and a regression with time series errors.

I find that, for every 1% increase in the price of Bitcoin, there is a corresponding 0.9135% increase in the price of Etherum. This confirms Bitcoin dominance in the cryptocurrency markets.
